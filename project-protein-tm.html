Projects / Protein Tm Prediction

Protein Melting Temperature Prediction via ESM and AlphaFold

Proteins · Multimodal · Regression

Workflow

Model Summary

Embeddings
ESM sequence and AlphaFold structure, 1280-d each.
Descriptors
Rosetta ΔG, Shannon entropy, OGT classification.
Fusion
Concatenation into a unified vector for the heads.
Heads
Auxiliary stability classifier (1280→256→32→Softmax) and an MLP regressor for Tm.
Data
28,463 proteins after CD-HIT 90%; 9:1 train/val; 75 post-2023 as test.
HPO
Optuna over LR, batch size, hidden size H, dropout, weight decay.
Results
Test RMSE = 9.37; 10-fold cross-validation ongoing.
