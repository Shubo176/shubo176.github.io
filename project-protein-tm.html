Summary

  • Embeddings: ESM (sequence) and AlphaFold (structure), both 1280-d.
  • Fusion with ΔG, entropy, OGT; MLP regressor with dropout.
  • De-redundant dataset of 28,463 proteins; temporal split for generalization.
  • Test RMSE = 9.37; conducting 10-fold cross-validation.

Notes and Next

  • Domain-aware OOD tests across protein families and temperature ranges.
  • Ablations on feature groups and stability-class auxiliary loss.
  • Interpretable residues via attribution on sequence/structure channels.
