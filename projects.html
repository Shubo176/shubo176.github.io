AI4S · Genomics

DNA Modification Detection from Nanopore Signals with Transformers

Multimodal input: standardized current traces (fixed length 32) and one-hot 21bp window fused into a 21×36 feature matrix. Modeled using a 6-layer Transformer encoder (8 heads, FFN 512→256→512, GELU) with residual connections, layer norm, and dropout.

  • Data: xtg103 (unmodified) and a fully modified plasmid for train/validation; sal87 for test.
  • Training: F1-driven LR scheduler, early stopping, best/last checkpoints.
  • Finding: ~20% modified proportion predicted on sal87 under current setting.
Pipeline details
  • Signal normalization to [-1, 1], window extraction, padding/truncation to length 32.
  • Sequence one-hot encoding for A/T/C/G and concatenation with signal features.
  • Average pooling over encoder outputs followed by an MLP classifier with logistic output.
AI4S · Proteins

Protein Melting Temperature Prediction via ESM and AlphaFold

Dual-channel fusion of sequence and structure embeddings (1280-d each), combined with Rosetta ΔG, Shannon entropy, and OGT classification outputs. Regressor is a multi-layer perceptron with dropout. Hyper-parameters selected with Optuna.

  • Data: FireProt, ProThermDB, Meltome, ThermomutDB, and literature-mined samples; CD-HIT 90% de-redundancy (N=28,463).
  • Split: 9:1 train/val. 75 post-2023 samples as a held-out test set.
  • Performance: test RMSE = 9.37; running 10-fold CV for stability assessment.
Model details
  • ESM embeddings reduced for auxiliary classification into low/mid/high stability by a 3-layer head.
  • Final regressor: Linear(din, H) → ReLU → Dropout → Linear(H, H/2) → ReLU → Dropout → Linear(H/2, 1).
